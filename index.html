About Me

I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Geosciences, University of Oslo, working on the CompSci Project. My research focuses on observing system simulation experiments (OSSE) through glacier observational data assimilation in Svalbard. I combine computational methods with glaciology to better understand and predict glacier behavior in a changing climate.

Research & Experience

Glacier Data Assimilation

Developing Bayesian data assimilation methods for Arctic glaciers, using large ensemble twin experiments to improve mass balance modeling in Svalbard.

Data Assimilation Bayesian Statistics

Cryosphere Field Research

Extensive fieldwork experience including thermal glacier investigation in Iceland, ice dynamics studies in Ny-Ålesund (Svalbard), and cryospheric monitoring in the Chilean Andes.
